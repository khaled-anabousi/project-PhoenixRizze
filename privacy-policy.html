Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 1, 2025

1. Introduction

At PhoenixRizze, we prioritize your privacy and data security. This policy aims to clarify how we collect, use, and protect data obtained through your use of our intelligent platform for generating feasibility studies.

We are committed to complying with data protection laws and regulations, including:

  • The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) in the European Union
  • The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), where applicable

2. Data We Collect

We collect only general, non-personal data, which includes:

  • Age (optional)
  • Gender (optional)
  • Platform usage data (session duration, number of studies generated, content types accessed)

We do not collect or store any directly identifiable personal information, such as:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Project or business idea details
  • Any other direct identifying information

3. Use of Data

We use this data for legitimate and documented purposes, including:

  • Improving platform performance through AI technologies
  • Supporting scientific and academic research related to feasibility studies
  • Monitoring and analyzing system usage to ensure quality and stability

Generated studies (without user identity linkage) are used to enhance our machine learning algorithms.

4. User Rights

Under applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data (if any exists)
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data or object to specific processing
  • Be informed about how and why your data is being used
Please contact us via the email below to exercise any of these rights.

5. Data Retention Period

We retain general data for up to 6 months from your last active use of the platform (e.g., generating or modifying a study), unless legal or operational requirements necessitate longer retention.

6. Data Protection

  • We use strong encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transmissions
  • Data is stored on secure servers with access controls
  • For international data transfers, we apply equivalent protection standards

7. Cookies

The platform uses cookies to enhance your experience, including:

  • Saving language preferences
  • Maintaining session continuity
  • Customizing the user interface

You can manage cookie preferences via your browser settings or our Cookie Settings page (link will be available soon).

8. Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties, except in these cases:

  • When required by legal authorities (we'll notify you unless prohibited)
  • With your explicit consent for specific purposes

9. Policy Changes

We may update this policy as needed. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notifications.

Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
